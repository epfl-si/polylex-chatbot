Rules and Regulations concerning Registrar LEX 2.10.4

Service Fees 15 March 2022

The Direction of the Ecole polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne,

based on Article 10 (3) of the Ordonnance, du Conseil des EPF, sur les taxes percues dans le

domaine des écoles polytechniques fédérales’ hereby adopts the following:

Article 1 Registrar Service Fees ' EPFL charges the following fees for services requested in connection with studies:

Preparation of a specific certificate 30.00

Certification of a copy of a document provided by 5.00 the person requesting it

Certification of a document issued by EPFL 15.00

2 The fees must be paid in advance. Otherwise, the service will not be provided.

Article 2 Entry into force These regulations enter into force on 15 March 2022.
