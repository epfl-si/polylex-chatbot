Rules and Regulations concerning Registrar Service Fees
15 March 2022
The Direction of the Ecole polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne, based on Article 10 (3) of the Ordonnance, du Conseil des EPF, sur les taxes perçues dans le domaine des écoles polytechniques fédérales!
hereby adopts the following:

Article 1 Registrar Service Fees
' EPFL charges the following fees for services requested in connection with studies:
Preparation of a specific certificate, Price in Swiss francs = 30.00. Certification of a copy of a document provided by the person requesting it, Price in Swiss francs = 5.00. Certification of a document issued by EPFL, Price in Swiss francs = 15.00. Legalization of a diploma, Price in Swiss francs = 5.00. Printing of a document (per 10 pages), Price in Swiss francs = 5.00. Sending by postal mail, Price in Swiss francs = 5.00. Sending by registered postal mail, Price in Swiss francs = 10.00. Replacement of EPFL ID card, Price in Swiss francs = in accordance with LEX 1.5.8?

Article 2 Entry into force
These regulations enter into force on 15 March 2022.
