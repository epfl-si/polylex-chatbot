






































Article 1


Rules and Regulations concerning Registrar  LEX 2.10.4 
Service Fees  
15 March 2022 
 

Version 1.0  1/1 

The Direction of the Ecole polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne, 
based on Article 10 (3) of the Ordonnance, du Conseil des EPF, sur les taxes perçues dans le 
domaine des écoles polytechniques fédérales1 
hereby adopts the following: 

Article 1 Registrar Service Fees 
1 EPFL charges the following fees for services requested in connection with studies: 

Service Price in Swiss francs 

Preparation of a specific certificate 30.00 

Certification of a copy of a document provided by 
the person requesting it 

5.00 

Certification of a document issued by EPFL 15.00 

Legalization of a diploma 5.00 

Printing of a document (per 10 pages) 5.00 

Sending by postal mail 5.00 

Sending by registered postal mail  10.00 

Replacement of EPFL ID card in accordance with LEX 1.5.82 

 
2 The fees must be paid in advance. Otherwise, the service will not be provided. 

Article 2 Entry into force  

These regulations enter into force on 15 March 2022.
